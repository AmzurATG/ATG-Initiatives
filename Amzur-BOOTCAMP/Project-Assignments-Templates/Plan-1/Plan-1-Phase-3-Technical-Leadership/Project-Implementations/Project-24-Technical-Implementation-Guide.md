# Project 24 - Building and Scaling AI/ML Engineering Team for Healthcare Startup - Technical Implementation Guide

## 1. Project Overview & Learning Objectives

### Business Context
Lead the organizational transformation of a healthcare AI startup from a 5-person engineering team to a 50+ person engineering organization over 18 months while maintaining innovative culture, technical excellence, and rapid development velocity. This project demonstrates technical leadership, organizational development, and scaling high-performance engineering teams in the healthcare AI domain.
