* **Global Expansion**: [Harvard Business Review - Global Strategy](https://hbr.org/topic/global-strategy) and [McKinsey Global Expansion Guide](https://www.mckinsey.com/business-functions/strategy-and-corporate-finance/our-insights)
* **Market Entry Strategy**: [BCG Market Entry](https://www.bcg.com/capabilities/strategy/market-entry) and [Bain International Expansion](https://www.bain.com/consulting-services/international-expansion/)
* **Cross-Cultural Management**: [Hofstede Cultural Dimensions](https://www.hofstede-insights.com/models/national-culture/) and [INSEAD Cultural Intelligence](https://www.insead.edu/executive-education/general-management/advanced-international-executive-programme)

### **Healthcare International Regulations**
* **EU Medical Device Regulation**: [MDR Implementation Guide](https://www.medical-device-regulation.eu/) and [EMA Guidelines](https://www.ema.europa.eu/en/human-regulatory/overview)
* **Asia-Pacific Regulations**: [PMDA Japan](https://www.pmda.go.jp/english/), [TGA Australia](https://www.tga.gov.au/), [HSA Singapore](https://www.hsa.gov.sg/)
* **International Standards**: [ISO 13485 Quality Management](https://www.iso.org/iso-13485-medical-devices.html) and [IEC 62304 Medical Device Software](https://www.iec.ch/dyn/www/f?p=103:38:0::::FSP_ORG_ID:1316)

### **Financial Planning and Risk Management**
* **International Finance**: [Corporate Finance Institute - International Finance](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/knowledge/finance/) and [Wharton International Finance](https://online.wharton.upenn.edu/courses/international-finance/)
* **Currency Risk Management**: [Treasury Risk Management](https://www.investopedia.com/articles/forex/11/managing-exchange-rate-risk.asp) and [Hedging Strategies](https://www.cmegroup.com/education/courses/introduction-to-fx/introduction-to-currency-hedging.html)
* **Political Risk Assessment**: [Political Risk Insurance](https://www.opic.gov/) and [Country Risk Analysis](https://www.eulerhermes.com/en_global/economic-research/country-risk.html)
