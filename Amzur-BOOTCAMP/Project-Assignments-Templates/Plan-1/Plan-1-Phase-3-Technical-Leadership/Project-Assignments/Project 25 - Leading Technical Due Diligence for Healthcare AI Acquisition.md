## **Objective (Why?)**

Lead the technical due diligence process for a $150M acquisition of a healthcare AI startup, evaluating technical assets, team capabilities, integration complexity, and post-acquisition value creation in 8-10 days. This project establishes your expertise in M&A technical leadership, strategic evaluation, and acquisition integration planning. You will practice:

* **Technical Due Diligence**: Comprehensive evaluation of technology assets, architecture, and technical debt
* **Risk Assessment**: Technical, operational, and strategic risk identification and mitigation planning
* **Value Creation**: Post-acquisition integration planning and synergy identification
* **Executive Communication**: Board-level presentations on technical findings and recommendations
* **Strategic Decision Making**: Contributing to acquisition decisions with technical expertise

**Challenge**: Assess risk level and create remediation plan without derailing acquisition
**Expected Response**: Risk assessment with priority-based remediation plan and cost estimates

### **Strategic Decision Points**

#### **Value Creation Opportunities**
- Technology synergies: AI platform consolidation vs. complementary capabilities
- Market expansion: Cross-selling to existing customers vs. new market entry
- Cost optimization: Infrastructure consolidation vs. operational efficiency
- Innovation acceleration: R&D integration vs. independent innovation labs

#### **Risk Mitigation Strategies**
- Key person risk: Talent retention strategies and knowledge transfer
- Customer retention: Integration communication and service continuity
- Regulatory compliance: FDA approval maintenance and expansion
- Technology obsolescence: Competitive technology assessment and roadmap

#### **Integration Approach Decisions**
- Integration speed: Fast integration for synergies vs. gradual integration for stability
- Technology consolidation: Platform unification vs. multi-product strategy
- Team integration: Full integration vs. autonomous subsidiary operation
- Cultural alignment: TechCorp culture adoption vs. startup culture preservation
