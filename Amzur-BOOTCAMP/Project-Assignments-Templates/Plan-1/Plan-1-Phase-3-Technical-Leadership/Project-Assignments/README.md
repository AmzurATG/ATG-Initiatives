# Track 4: Technical Leadership & Team Management

## Track Overview

**Target Outcome**: Transform advanced AI/ML engineers into technical leaders and engineering managers capable of building, scaling, and leading high-performing AI/ML engineering teams at healthcare startups, mid-stage companies, and Fortune 500 organizations.

**Duration**: 15-20 days  
**Team Structure**: 3-4 participants working as leadership team with rotating management responsibilities and peer coaching  
**Client Simulation**: Real organizational challenges including team building, performance management, technical strategy, and cross-functional leadership

## Core Competencies Development

### **Technical Team Leadership**
- **Team Building & Scaling**: Recruiting, hiring, onboarding, and scaling AI/ML engineering teams from startup to enterprise scale
- **Performance Management**: Setting expectations, providing feedback, conducting performance reviews, and managing career development for technical talent
- **Technical Mentoring**: Developing junior and mid-level engineers through coaching, pair programming, code reviews, and professional development planning
- **Engineering Culture**: Building and maintaining high-performance engineering culture including psychological safety, innovation, and continuous improvement

### **Organizational Development**
- **Technical Strategy**: Long-term technical vision, architecture roadmapping, and technology investment planning for organizational growth
- **Cross-functional Leadership**: Leading collaboration between engineering, product, business, clinical, and regulatory teams in healthcare organizations
- **Change Management**: Managing organizational change including technology adoption, process improvement, and cultural transformation
- **Talent Development**: Building comprehensive talent development programs including training, mentorship, and career progression frameworks

### **Executive Leadership & Communication**
- **Strategic Planning**: Contributing to organizational strategy including business planning, competitive positioning, and market expansion
- **Executive Communication**: Board-level reporting on technical strategy, team performance, and engineering organization health
- **Stakeholder Management**: Managing relationships with diverse stakeholders including executives, investors, customers, and regulatory bodies
- **Budget & Resource Management**: Technical budget planning, resource allocation, and ROI optimization for engineering investments

## Project Portfolio (3 Core Projects)

### **Project 24: Building and Scaling AI/ML Engineering Team for Healthcare Startup**
**Duration**: 6-7 days  
**Client**: Healthcare AI Startup (Series A/B simulation)  
**Business Challenge**: Scale engineering team from 5 to 50+ engineers while maintaining culture, quality, and innovation velocity

#### **Client Scenario**
*"Our healthcare AI startup has achieved product-market fit and secured Series B funding. We need to scale our engineering team from 5 to 50+ engineers over 18 months while maintaining our innovative culture, technical excellence, and rapid development velocity. Lead the organizational transformation including hiring strategy, team structure, processes, and culture development."*

#### **Core Requirements**
- **Hiring Strategy**: Comprehensive talent acquisition strategy including role definitions, interview processes, compensation frameworks, and diversity initiatives
- **Team Structure**: Organizational design including team topology, reporting structures, communication patterns, and decision-making frameworks
- **Engineering Processes**: Scalable engineering processes including agile methodologies, code review, testing, deployment, and incident management
- **Culture Development**: Strategies for maintaining startup culture while adding enterprise-grade processes and organizational structure
- **Technical Leadership**: Development of technical leadership pipeline including senior engineer growth, tech leads, and engineering management roles

#### **Leadership Deliverables**
- **Hiring Plan**: Comprehensive 18-month hiring plan including role priorities, sourcing strategies, interview processes, and compensation benchmarks
- **Organizational Design**: Team structure and organizational chart including reporting relationships, communication patterns, and decision-making authority
- **Process Framework**: Engineering process documentation including development workflows, quality standards, and operational procedures
- **Culture Strategy**: Culture development plan including values definition, behavior modeling, and culture measurement frameworks
- **Leadership Development**: Technical leadership development program including mentoring, career progression, and succession planning

#### **Operational Deliverables**
- **Performance Framework**: Performance management system including goal setting, feedback mechanisms, and career development planning
- **Communication Strategy**: Internal communication framework including team meetings, documentation standards, and cross-functional collaboration
- **Technology Strategy**: Technical roadmap including architecture evolution, technology adoption, and technical debt management
- **Quality Assurance**: Quality frameworks including code standards, testing strategies, security practices, and compliance requirements
- **Growth Planning**: Scalability planning for continued growth including space, infrastructure, and organizational development
