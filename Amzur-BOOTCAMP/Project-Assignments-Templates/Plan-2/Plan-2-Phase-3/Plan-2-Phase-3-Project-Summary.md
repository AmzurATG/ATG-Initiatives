1. **Client Discovery & Requirements** (Week 1: Days 1-2)
   - Stakeholder interviews and requirement gathering
   - Business case development and success metrics
   - Technical feasibility assessment and architecture planning

   - Continuous integration and deployment

   - Production deployment and performance validation
   - Client demo and stakeholder presentation
   - Project retrospective and lessons learned

   - Performance optimization and scalability

   - Demo preparation and technical presentation
