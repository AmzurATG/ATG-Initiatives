# Lesson Plan Assignment: Vertical-Specific Solutions

---

## Assignment Information

**Category:** Category 11: Domain-Specific Applications  
**Subcategory:** 11.2 Vertical-Specific Solutions  
**Assigned To:** [Team Member 1 & Team Member 2]  
**Presentation Date:** [Date]  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Understand AI applications in healthcare, finance, and retail
2. Navigate industry-specific regulations and requirements
3. Design vertical-specific AI solutions

---

## Topics to Cover

- **Healthcare AI:** Medical records, diagnosis support, regulatory compliance (HIPAA)
- **Financial Services:** Fraud detection, risk assessment, compliance (SOX, regulations)
- **E-commerce & Retail:** Personalization, inventory, customer insights
- **Legal & Compliance:** Contract analysis, legal research, compliance monitoring

---

## Assignment Deliverables

### 1. Presentation (Required)
- Create slides on AI applications by industry vertical
- Include regulatory requirements and constraints
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

### 2. Code Demo/Example (Required)
- Build vertical-specific AI solution (choose 1-2 industries)
- Demonstrate compliance-aware implementation
- Show industry-specific features and workflows

### 3. Resources & References (Required)
- Curate 3-5 resources per vertical
- Create regulatory compliance checklists
- Share vertical-specific solution patterns

### 4. Q&A Session (Required)
- Prepare for 10-15 minutes of questions
- Discussion questions:
  - "Which verticals are opportunities for ATG?"
  - "How do we build domain expertise?"
