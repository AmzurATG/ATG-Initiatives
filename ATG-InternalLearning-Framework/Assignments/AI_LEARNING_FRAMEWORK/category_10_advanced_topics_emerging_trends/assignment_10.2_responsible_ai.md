# Lesson Plan Assignment: Responsible AI

---

## Assignment Information

**Category:** Category 10: Advanced Topics & Emerging Trends  
**Subcategory:** 10.2 Responsible AI  
**Assigned To:** [Team Member 1 & Team Member 2]  
**Presentation Date:** [Date]  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Understand AI ethics and fairness principles
2. Detect and mitigate bias in AI systems
3. Implement explainability and interpretability techniques

---

## Topics to Cover

- **AI Ethics & Fairness:** Ethical principles, fairness metrics, responsible deployment
- **Bias Detection & Mitigation:** Types of bias, detection methods, mitigation strategies
- **Explainability & Interpretability:** LIME, SHAP, explaining AI decisions
- **AI Governance:** Policies, oversight, accountability frameworks

---

## Assignment Deliverables

### 1. Presentation (Required)
- Create slides on responsible AI principles and practices
- Include real-world examples of bias and mitigation
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

### 2. Code Demo/Example (Required)
- Demonstrate bias detection in AI system
- Show explainability techniques (LIME/SHAP)
- Implement fairness metrics evaluation

### 3. Resources & References (Required)
- Curate 3-5 resources on responsible AI
- Create responsible AI checklist for projects
- Share tools for bias detection and explainability

### 4. Q&A Session (Required)
- Prepare for 10-15 minutes of questions
- Discussion questions:
  - "How do we ensure our AI systems are fair?"
  - "What are our ethical responsibilities?"

---

## Preparation Guidelines

- **Research:** Study AI ethics frameworks and guidelines
- **Practice:** Analyze systems for bias and explainability
- **Collaborate:** One covers ethics/bias, other demonstrates tools
- **Engage:** Review team projects through ethical lens
