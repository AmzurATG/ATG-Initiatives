## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Understand RLHF and its role in LLM training
2. Explain quantization and optimization techniques
3. Recognize emerging techniques like mixture of experts

---

## Topics to Cover

- **RLHF:** Reinforcement learning from human feedback, how it improves models
- **Constitutional AI:** AI alignment, safety constraints, guided behavior
- **Mixture of Experts:** MoE architecture, efficiency benefits
- **Quantization & Optimization:** Reducing model size, inference optimization
