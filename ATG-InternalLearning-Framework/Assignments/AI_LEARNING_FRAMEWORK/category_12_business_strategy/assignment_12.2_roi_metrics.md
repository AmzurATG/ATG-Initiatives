# Lesson Plan Assignment: ROI & Metrics

---

## Assignment Information

**Category:** Category 12: Business & Strategy  
**Subcategory:** 12.2 ROI & Metrics  
**Assigned To:** [Team Member 1 & Team Member 2]  
**Presentation Date:** [Date]  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Measure AI system success with appropriate metrics
2. Conduct cost-benefit analysis for AI investments
3. Demonstrate business value to stakeholders

---

## Topics to Cover

- **Measuring AI Success:** KPIs, success metrics, baseline establishment
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis:** Total cost of ownership, ROI calculation, payback period
- **Performance Metrics:** Technical metrics, business metrics, user satisfaction
- **Business Value Demonstration:** Reporting, dashboards, stakeholder communication

---

## Assignment Deliverables

### 1. Presentation (Required)
- Create slides on AI metrics and ROI frameworks
- Include real ROI calculations and examples
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

### 2. Code Demo/Example (Required)
- Build metrics tracking dashboard
- Demonstrate ROI calculation tool
- Show business value reporting

### 3. Resources & References (Required)
- Curate 3-5 resources on AI metrics and ROI
- Create ROI calculation templates
- Share metrics tracking frameworks

### 4. Q&A Session (Required)
- Prepare for 10-15 minutes of questions
- Discussion questions:
  - "How do we prove ROI to clients?"
  - "What metrics matter most?"

## Preparation Guidelines

- **Research:** Study AI ROI case studies and methodologies
- **Practice:** Calculate ROI for real or hypothetical projects
- **Collaborate:** One covers metrics, other demonstrates ROI analysis
- **Engage:** Apply to ATG projects and proposals
