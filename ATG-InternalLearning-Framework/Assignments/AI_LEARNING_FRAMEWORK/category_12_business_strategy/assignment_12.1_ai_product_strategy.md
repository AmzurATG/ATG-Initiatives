# Lesson Plan Assignment: AI Product Strategy

---

## Assignment Information

**Category:** Category 12: Business & Strategy  
**Subcategory:** 12.1 AI Product Strategy  
**Assigned To:** [Team Member 1 & Team Member 2]  
**Presentation Date:** [Date]  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Make informed build vs buy decisions for AI products
2. Develop effective MVP strategies for AI products
3. Achieve product-market fit through iteration

---

## Topics to Cover

- **Build vs Buy Decisions:** When to build custom vs use existing solutions
- **MVP Development:** Minimum viable product for AI, rapid prototyping
- **User Feedback & Iteration:** Collecting feedback, iterative improvement
- **Product-Market Fit:** Finding PMF for AI products, validation strategies

---

## Assignment Deliverables

### 1. Presentation (Required)
- Create slides on AI product strategy frameworks
- Include decision trees and evaluation criteria
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

### 2. Code Demo/Example (Required)
- Demonstrate MVP development approach
- Show feedback collection and iteration cycle
- Build simple AI product prototype

### 3. Resources & References (Required)
- Curate 3-5 resources on AI product management
- Create build vs buy decision framework
- Share MVP and iteration templates

### 4. Q&A Session (Required)
- Prepare for 10-15 minutes of questions
- Discussion questions:
  - "How do we validate AI product ideas quickly?"
  - "What's our product development process?"
