# Lesson Plan Assignment: Client Engagement

---

## Assignment Information

**Category:** Category 12: Business & Strategy  
**Subcategory:** 12.3 Client Engagement  
**Assigned To:** [Team Member 1 & Team Member 2]  
**Presentation Date:** [Date]  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, team members should be able to:
1. Respond effectively to AI-related RFPs
2. Develop compelling demos and POCs
3. Communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders

---

## Topics to Cover

- **RFP Response Strategies:** Understanding requirements, proposal structure, differentiation
- **Demo & POC Development:** Effective demos, POC scoping, success criteria
- **Technical Proposal Writing:** Translating technical to business language
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Executive presentations, managing expectations

---

## Assignment Deliverables

### 1. Presentation (Required)
- Create slides on client engagement best practices
- Include sample RFP responses and demo strategies
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

### 2. Code Demo/Example (Required)
- Build compelling AI demo for client scenarios
- Demonstrate POC development process
- Show effective technical presentation

### 3. Resources & References (Required)
- Curate 3-5 resources on client engagement
- Create RFP response and demo templates
- Share stakeholder communication frameworks

### 4. Q&A Session (Required)
- Prepare for 10-15 minutes of questions
- Discussion questions:
  - "How do we differentiate in competitive RFPs?"
  - "What makes an effective AI demo?"

---

## Preparation Guidelines

- **Research:** Study successful proposals and demos
- **Practice:** Create sample RFP response and demo
- **Collaborate:** One covers proposals, other demonstrates demos
- **Engage:** Role-play client presentations
