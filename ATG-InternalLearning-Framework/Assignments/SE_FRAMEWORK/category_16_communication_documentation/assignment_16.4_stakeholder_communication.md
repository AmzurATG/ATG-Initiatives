## Learning Objectives
By the end of this session, the team should be able to:
1. Communicate effectively with different stakeholders
2. Present technical information to non-technical audiences
3. Manage stakeholder expectations
4. Handle difficult conversations

---

## Topics to Cover

### 1. Stakeholder Types
- Technical stakeholders (developers, architects)
- Business stakeholders (product managers, executives)
- Customers and end users
- Support and operations teams
- Understanding stakeholder needs
- Communication preferences

### 2. Translating Technical to Business
- Business value focus
- Avoiding technical jargon
- Using analogies and metaphors
- Visual communication
- Impact and outcome emphasis
- Cost-benefit framing

### 3. Expectation Management
- Setting realistic expectations
- Progress updates
- Scope changes communication
- Risk and issue escalation
- Timeline communication
- Saying no constructively

### 4. Difficult Conversations
- Delivering bad news
- Managing conflicts
- Handling criticism
- Active listening
- Empathy and understanding
- Solution-focused approach
